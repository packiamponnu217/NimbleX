Q. Will I work on projects?
Yes. Project-based learning is a core part of the program.
Q. How is progress tracked?
Attendance, assignments, projects, assessments, and learning milestones are continuously monitored through our progress tracking system.

Students, graduates, career switchers, and anyone looking to build strong software engineering skills.
