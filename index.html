FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Do I need prior programming knowledge?
No. We start from fundamentals and gradually advance to full-stack and AI concepts.
Q. How is NimbleX different from other platforms?
Every Engineer receives personalized guidance through Architects and Reporting Managers, ensuring consistent progress and accountability.
Q. Will I work on projects?
Yes. Project-based learning is a core part of the program.
Q. How is progress tracked?
Attendance, assignments, projects, assessments, and learning milestones are continuously monitored through our progress tracking system.
Q. Who should join NimbleX?
Students, graduates, career switchers, and anyone looking to build strong software engineering skills.
